ICHA TEA

Icha tea is the perfect place to wind down with your friends. They have authentic tea that will wind you down and have a peaceful time.

Not only that it is great catching up, it was also really great for those who want to study but it could get busy after 3pm.

A huge plus for dog owners is that it is dog friendly but not as much during busy times where it can get a bit squishy.
